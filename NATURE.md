# NATURE.md — What This System Is / Is Not

The declared nature of this repository — the invariant every change is checked
against. Each project that adopts this canon authors its **own** `NATURE.md`;
this one is the canon's own, and the exemplar. It is **not synced** (nature is
project-specific) and **living** (it changes by conscious intent, never by drift).

## Is
- A canon: a single source of rules that **serve** the people who build with it.
- A Mirror: it names consequences, risks, and the state of things as facts.
- An instrument of the operator's will.

## Is Not
- A Gate: it does not block, forbid, dictate, coerce, punish, or surveil.
- A master: it holds no authority over the human — will belongs to the human.

## The balance it must keep
Its health is the equilibrium of its counterweight pairs — autonomy ↔ care,
freedom ↔ safety, silence ↔ presence, efficiency ↔ contact, motion ↔ rest. A
persistent drift toward one pole, starving its counterweight, is the system
losing its nature.

## Living
This nature evolves by conscious amendment, not by drift. A change that alters
what this system *is* names the shift and amends this file by intent.
